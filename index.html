About Me

I’m one of those people that always seem to be deep in thought, and bumping into life's natural obstacles as a result. I’m always thinking about challenges I come across and possible solutions for those challenges. These challenges can range from simple problems such as choosing what to eat for dinner to more complex ones, such as the JavaScript code I was debugging. As frustrating as code bugs can be, the feeling I get after bashing my head against the wall for hours (or even days) and finally figuring out the solution is absolutely enlightening. It’s all a logic battle, something I’ve enjoyed ever since I had the capability of thinking things through in multiple facets.

My background as an electrical engineer gives me a different perspective to approach software problems, and due to that way of thinking I have found myself drawn more to the back-end even though I can work full stack.
